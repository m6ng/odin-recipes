Scrambed Eggs

Description

Classic recipe of scrambled eggs!

Ingredients

  • A few eggs
  • A little bit of sliced Cheddar cheese
  • A few thin slices of ham (Optional)
  • A little bit of heavy whipping cream
  • A little bit of butter

Steps

  1. Whisk the eggs together in a small bowl until smooth. Mix in Cheddar cheese, ham, and heavy cream.
  2. Melt butter in a skillet over medium heat. Pour in egg mixture; cook and stir until set but still moist, 3 to 5 minutes.
